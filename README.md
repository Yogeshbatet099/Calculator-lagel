# Privacy Policy for Calculator Pro

**Last Updated:** February 2026

## Introduction

Calculator Pro ("we," "our," or "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our calculator application.

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Calculator Pro stores the following data locally on your device:

- **Calculation History:** Your mathematical calculations and results are saved locally using your device's storage (SharedPreferences)
- **User Preferences:** App settings and preferences you configure
- **Currency Converter Cache:** Exchange rates are temporarily cached on your device for offline functionality

**Important:** All data is stored only on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers.

### Information Collected Through Third-Party Services

**Currency Exchange Rates:** We use the Frankfurter API (https://www.frankfurter.app) to fetch real-time currency exchange rates. When you use the Currency Converter feature:
- Your device sends requests to Frankfurter API servers
- No personal information is transmitted
- Only currency conversion requests are made
- Frankfurter API has its own privacy policy available at their website

### Information We DO NOT Collect

We do not collect:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics or statistics
- Crash reports or diagnostic data
- Any information from your device contacts, photos, or files

## How We Use Information

The locally stored data is used solely to:
- Display your calculation history within the app
- Maintain your app preferences and settings
- Provide offline currency exchange rates (cached data)
- Enhance your user experience

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage mechanisms
- No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers (except currency API requests)
- Your calculation history remains completely private on your device
- When you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is automatically deleted

## Third-Party Services

Calculator Pro uses the following third-party service:

**Frankfurter API** - For currency exchange rates
- Website: https://www.frankfurter.app
- This service may collect basic request metadata (IP address, request time)
- We recommend reviewing their privacy policy for more information

## Children's Privacy

Calculator Pro is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special restrictions or parental consent requirements.

## Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:
- Clearing calculation history from within the app
- Clearing app data from your device's Settings > Apps > Calculator Pro > Storage > Clear Data
- Uninstalling the app (this removes all stored data)

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your locally stored data through the app interface
- Delete your data at any time
- Stop using the app if you disagree with this policy
